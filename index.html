Cheatsheet & Service Discovery Tips

  • Inspect a service: curl -I <endpoint>
  • Check DNS info: nslookup <domain>
  • Monitor logs: tail -f /var/log/nginx/access.log
  • Auto-refresh: Press F5 or wait for periodic updates.
  • Service discovery: Review the "Discovery" field for auto/manual configuration details.
